# News Summarizer

This is a simple website that fetches the latest news articles based on user-defined parameters (source, topic, and timeline) and provides a summary.

## Features:
- **Any News Mode**: Get a general news summary for the last 24 hours or 7 days.
- **Custom News Mode**: Specify a topic, and the app will fetch relevant news articles from the user-defined sources.
- Summaries are generated based on articles fetched from multiple sources.
- Clean, modern interface.
